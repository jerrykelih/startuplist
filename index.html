About StartupList.no

Why?

When you are working on your startup you are focused on your baby. Often to busy to hear about someone elses baby. But what if there is someone else working on something related to what you are. Wouldn't it be nice to talk and maybe share ideas. Maybe someone is working on a technology that you can use instead of developing it yourself.
You are in a coworking space, but do you know what the guy next to you is doing ? Do you know what people in other coworking spaces are doing ?

How?

There are lists of startups, but they tend to be outdated. Several reasons for this failure. One is that they are maintained by someone that after a while stops maintaining it. Therefore we created a list that is maintained by the entrepreneurs themselves.

Open!

The startuplist will have a RSS feed that can be used to pull the data in the list so that anyone can put updated information about startups on their site. YOU are now in control of what is written about your startup.
